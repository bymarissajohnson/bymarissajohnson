Daily Routines

☀️ Morning Routine
1
FAB Pure Skin Face Cleanser
Gentle rinse-off. Massage 30 sec, rinse with lukewarm water. Pat dry — do not rub.
2
Medicube Azelaic Acid Toner
Apply with hands or cotton pad while skin is still slightly damp. Gently press in.
3
Bambu Earth Vitamin C Serum
2–3 drops. Press into skin. This fights hyperpigmentation + adds morning glow.
4
Medicube Exosome Shot 700
1–2 drops. Layer over Vit C. Firms and helps skin repair. Allow to absorb 30–60 sec.
5
Drunk Elephant B-Hydra Serum
2–3 pumps over serums. Locks in moisture and preps skin for moisturizer.
6
⚡ Booster Pro Device
Use NOW — after hydration serum. Microcurrent/LED mode glides over serums. Helps actives penetrate deeper. 3–5 minutes.
7
Aestura Atobarrier 365 Cream
Seal everything in. Focus on dry areas. Great barrier moisturizer for sensitive skin.
8
☀️ SPF 30–50 (Separate)
Always finish AM routine with SPF — essential for hyperpigmentation and aging. Choose a mineral SPF for sensitive skin.
🌙 Evening Routine
1
Medicube Cleansing Oil (Double Cleanse Step 1)
Massage onto dry face. Breaks down sunscreen & makeup. Emulsify with water, rinse.
2
FAB Pure Skin Cleanser (Step 2) — or PanOxyl 2–3x/week
Regular nights: FAB cleanser. Mon/Wed/Fri: Sub PanOxyl — leave on 1–2 min max, rinse. Do NOT use both in one night.
3
Medicube Azelaic Acid Toner
Same as AM. Preps skin for treatments.
4
Medicube Azelaic Acid Peptide Repair
Targeted on hyperpigmentation, acne spots. Allow to absorb 1 min before next step.
5
Drunk Elephant B-Hydra Serum
Replenish moisture and prep for retinoid nights.
6
⚡ Booster Pro Device (non-Differin nights only)
Skip device on Differin nights. Other PM nights: use after B-Hydra serum, before moisturizer. LED mode great for PM repair. 3–5 min.
7
Differin Adapalene (Tue/Thu/Sat only)
Thin layer on entire face. Wait 20 min after B-Hydra before applying. Only on these nights — skip if skin is irritated.
8
Tatcha Polypeptide Cream
Richer PM moisturizer. Supports overnight repair and firmness.
9
Hossance Squalane + Vit C Rose Oil (optional PM)
2–3 drops pressed on top of moisturizer. Seals everything, adds overnight glow. Skip on Differin nights (weeks 1–4).

Key Notes

⚠️ Sensitive Skin Warnings
  • Never use PanOxyl + Differin on the same night — too irritating
  • Skip Vitamin C if skin feels reactive — use Exosome instead
  • Weeks 1–2: Use Differin only 1x/week until skin adjusts
  • If purging from Differin: this is normal for up to 6 weeks
  • Skip Booster Pro on any nights you use Differin
  • Don't layer Tatcha Water Cream + Polypeptide Cream — choose one per session
💧 Hydration Tips
  • Apply all serums to damp skin for better absorption
  • B-Hydra Serum is your hydration hero — use every AM + PM
  • Atobarrier 365 is ideal for AM (lighter) — Polypeptide Cream for PM (richer)
  • If very dry, you can layer Tatcha Water Cream under Polypeptide at night
  • Drink 2L of water daily — it shows on your skin within weeks
✨ Glow & Brightening
  • Vitamin C (Bambu Earth) + Azelaic Acid Toner = powerful hyperpigmentation duo
  • Exosome Shot 700 + Booster Pro = maximum glow penetration
  • Squalane oil at night adds incredible luminosity by morning
  • Results for hyperpigmentation take 6–8 weeks of consistent use
  • SPF in the morning is NON-NEGOTIABLE to prevent new dark spots
📅 Tatcha Cream Usage
  • Tatcha Dewy Skin Cream: Use as PM moisturizer on non-Differin nights as a treat/alternate
  • Tatcha Water Cream: Lighter — great for AM if you find Atobarrier too rich some days
  • Rotate them to prevent product fatigue and keep skin guessing
  • Polypeptide Cream is priority — most firming/anti-aging of the three
