Guide Preview

3 Days in Tokyo

A peek at what's inside the free Tokyo guide — your day-by-day itinerary built from my actual trips.

Day 1
Arrival & East Tokyo
Morning
Asakusa & Senso-ji Temple
Arrive early (before 9am) to beat the crowds. The temple grounds at dawn are magical.
✦ Stop at Nakamise shopping street for traditional snacks
Afternoon
Akihabara → Ueno
Electronics district, anime culture, then Ueno Park and museums.
Evening
Ramen dinner in Ikebukuro
Ramen street has multiple styles in one building. Try at least two.
✦ Get there before 6pm to avoid long waits
Day 2
Culture & Fashion
Morning
Harajuku & Meiji Shrine
Start at the serene shrine then walk to Takeshita Street for Harajuku fashion and crepes.
Afternoon
Shibuya Crossing & Omotesando
Do the famous crossing, then walk up to Omotesando for high-end shopping and architecture.
✦ Shibuya Sky rooftop observation deck for views
Evening
Shinjuku Golden Gai
Tiny bars, local vibes, unforgettable atmosphere. Order a drink and make friends.
Day 3
Food & Hidden Gems
Morning
Tsukiji Outer Market
Best sushi and fresh seafood breakfast you will ever have. Get there by 8am.
✦ Tuna sashimi for breakfast changes your life
Afternoon
Shimokitazawa
Tokyo's coolest neighborhood. Vintage shops, indie cafes, live music venues. Feels like a different city.
Evening
Izakaya farewell dinner
Find a neighborhood izakaya, order everything on the menu, and celebrate a perfect Tokyo trip.
