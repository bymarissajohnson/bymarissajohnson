My Story

Real life.
All of it.

Hi, I'm Marissa — a media personality, attorney, Navy veteran, and content creator who has never believed you have to fit into just one box. You might know me as a fan favorite from Netflix's Love is Blind Season 7, or from Variety Magazine's 30 Most Powerful Women of Reality TV (2024). But honestly, TV is just one chapter of a much bigger story.

I served in the United States Navy, earned my law degree, co-host Legally Speaking on Fox 5 DC, and somehow also ended up sharing my heart on national television. I am also set to appear in another major Netflix reality series in 2026 — so there is always more coming.

"I am driven by connection, purpose, and the belief that you can be strong and soft at the same time."

Right now I am living in Japan as a military spouse, newly married to my absolute best friend, with my roots in Washington DC. My life looks nothing like I planned — and I mean that in the best possible way. I have lived adventurously, loved deeply, and learned to trust that the unconventional path is often the most meaningful one.

At the center of everything I do is a deep desire to make people feel seen and inspired. I care about authenticity, integrity, and building a life that actually means something. I influence people to live their best lives — most true to themselves. And yes, I am absolutely the person who will pull you into a deep conversation, debate pop culture for two hours, and still make time for a little romance. 🌸
