My Story

Real life.
All of it.

Marissa Johnson is a nationally recognized media personality, attorney, and content creator, named one of Variety Magazine's 30 Most Powerful Women of Reality TV (2024). She rose to prominence as a fan favorite on Netflix's hit reality series Love is Blind Season 7 and continues to expand her presence across entertainment, lifestyle, beauty, wellness, and legal commentary. She is also set to appear in another major Netflix reality series scheduled for release in 2026.

With a background as a Navy veteran and lawyer, Marissa brings credibility, relatability, and trust to every partnership. Her content blends aspirational lifestyle with real-life storytelling, making her a powerful brand partner for companies seeking both influence and impact.

"I am driven by connection, purpose, and the belief that you can be strong and soft at the same time."

Currently based in Japan as a military spouse, with roots in Washington DC, Marissa is newly married to her best friend and living proof that life does not follow a straight line — and that's exactly the point. She served her country, earned her law degree, and somehow also found herself sharing her heart on national television.

At the center of all of it is someone who cares deeply — about people feeling seen, about living with integrity, and about building a life that feels meaningful and honest. She is the kind of person who loves a deep conversation, a little romance, and a good pop culture debate.
