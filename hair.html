Curl Education

Understanding porosity

The most important thing nobody taught us about curly hair — and why it changes everything.

⬇️
Low Porosity

Resistant to moisture

  • Takes forever to get wet
  • Products sit on top
  • Needs heat to open cuticle
  • Prone to buildup
  • Use lightweight, liquid-based products
⚖️
Normal Porosity

The sweet spot

  • Absorbs moisture easily
  • Holds styles and color well
  • Most products work well
  • Minimal maintenance needed
  • Focus on maintaining health
⬆️
High Porosity

Absorbs fast, loses fast

  • Gets wet immediately
  • Dries quickly but feels dry
  • Needs heavy sealers (oils, butters)
  • Benefits from protein treatments
  • Use the LOC or LCO method
