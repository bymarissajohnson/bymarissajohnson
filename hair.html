Curl Education

Understanding porosity

The most important thing nobody taught us about curly hair.

⬇️
Low Porosity

Resistant to moisture

  • Takes forever to wet
  • Products sit on top
  • Needs heat to open cuticle
  • Prone to buildup
  • Use lightweight water-based products
⚖️
Normal Porosity

The sweet spot

  • Absorbs moisture easily
  • Holds styles well
  • Most products work
  • Minimal maintenance
  • Focus on maintaining health
⬆️
High Porosity

Absorbs fast, loses fast

  • Gets wet immediately
  • Loses moisture quickly
  • Needs heavy sealers
  • Benefits from protein
  • Use LOC or LCO method
