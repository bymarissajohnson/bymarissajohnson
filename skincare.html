Marissa's Tips

What's actually working

01

Double cleanse every single night

Cleansing oil first to melt sunscreen and makeup, then your gentle cleanser. This one habit cleared my skin more than any serum did.

02

Apply serums to damp skin

Slightly damp skin absorbs active ingredients 40% more efficiently. Pat dry after cleansing but don't wait — apply within 60 seconds.

03

SPF is your #1 anti-aging tool

No vitamin C, retinol, or expensive serum will work if you skip SPF. It also prevents new hyperpigmentation — non-negotiable for me.

04

Be patient with retinoids

Purging from Differin is real and it's temporary — usually 4–6 weeks. Most people quit right before it starts working. Stay consistent.
